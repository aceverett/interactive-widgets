Recruitment
Obtaining the "right" number of program participants with appropriate eligibility characteristics to participate in needed comparison groups
Mandated
People who are required to participate in a program
Retention
Maximizing the number of participants who continue the intervention throughout the evaluation period and who are willing to provide whatever follow-up information is needed by the evaluators
Attrition
Number of people who leave a program (lost to follow-up)
